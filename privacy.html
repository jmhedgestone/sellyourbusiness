1. Definition of Personal Data and Non-Personal Data

Personal Data means any information that identifies or can be used to identify an individual, including but not limited to name, email address, phone number, and business information submitted through this site.

Non-Personal Data means demographic or usage statistics that cannot reasonably be used to identify a specific individual.

2. Scope

This policy governs the collection, use, and protection of data submitted through this website and any associated platform.

3. Data Collected

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Identity data — your name and business name

Information you provide is used to:

Your personal data is not sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes without your explicit consent. We may share data as required by law or legal process.

Aggregated, anonymized data may be used for trend analysis and may be shared with partners and advertisers in aggregate form only.

Certain hardware and software information is recorded automatically to identify abuse, resolve technical issues, and optimize site performance. This data does not personally identify you.

Data is retained as long as needed to provide services, satisfy legal obligations, or support legal claims.
